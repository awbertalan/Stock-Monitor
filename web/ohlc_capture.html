Why we're collecting this

The existing daily *_History.csv files store close prices only — no open/high/low. Candlestick patterns (Hammer, Engulfing, Morning Star, etc.) are defined by within-bar geometry and are impossible to detect from closes alone. Starting 2026-05-23, every trading day's EOD sweep captures a fresh OHLC bar from the Millistream live quote endpoint (which exposes openprice / dayhighprice / daylowprice / lastprice / quantity) and appends it to a per-stock CSV in ohlc/. Once ~30 bars per stock have accumulated (early July 2026) the candlestick-pattern engine can be built on top, running parallel to Live Signals with a separate paper wallet. See Live Signals help for the planned engine specifics (TP +0.5% / SL −2% / max 3-day hold, no-Friday, top-6 bullish patterns).
