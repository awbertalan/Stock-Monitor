Strategy ★ Tab

A pre-filtered screener that automatically surfaces stocks matching a backtested intraday strategy. Only equities that pass all three conditions appear here.

DoD > 1% Today's price is more than 1% above yesterday's closing price — the stock has momentum.
Not at high The stock is not within 0.5% of its 5-day high — avoids chasing exhausted moves.
Equity only Indices, funds, and other instrument types are excluded.
Entry rule: Buy at next open · Mon–Thu only (no Friday entries) · Target +2% · Stop −1% · Hold max 7 days.
Backtested results (10+ years): 40.9% win rate · avg +72 SEK per trade · profitable in 10 of 11 years. Best performers: SEB A, Nordea, SKF B, Electrolux B. Skip H&M B.

Simulation Tab

The Simulation tab is a paper trading journal — a way to test the strategy with real market data but without risking real money. You log a stock as an entry at its current price, and the app automatically tracks the outcome over the next 7 days using intraday price data it already has. All trades are saved to disk so the history persists across restarts.

How a trade works — step by step
  • 1️⃣ Find a candidate — go to the Strategy ★ tab after market open. The screener has already filtered for DoD > 1%, not at 5d high, equity only.
  • 2️⃣ Log the entry — hover over the stock card and click + Sim. The app records the exact current price as your entry price, along with the timestamp.
  • 3️⃣ Automatic target & stop — the app immediately calculates your target at entry × 1.02 (+2%) and stop at entry × 0.99 (−1%). These are shown in the trade table.
  • 4️⃣ First-to-hit detection — on every page load, the app scans the stock's 7-day intraday CSV tick by tick from your entry timestamp forward. It records the exact moment either the target or the stop was first touched. Whichever comes first wins — just like a real bracket order.
  • 5️⃣ T+7 close price — after exactly 7 calendar days, the app also records the closing price from the History CSV. This tells you where the stock ended up regardless of whether target/stop triggered — useful for seeing the full move.
  • 6️⃣ Trade closes — once T+7 has passed the trade moves from Open to Closed, and the outcome (✓ Target hit / ✗ Stop hit / Timeout) is locked in.
Reading the results table
✓ Target hit
Price reached +2% before −1%. Counted as a win. The exact candle where it triggered is recorded.
✗ Stop hit
Price fell to −1% before reaching +2%. Counted as a loss. Your downside was limited to 1% of entry.
Timeout
Neither target nor stop was hit in 7 days. The T+7 closing price determines win/loss — if it's above entry it's a win, below it's a loss.
T+7 P&L %
The percentage change from entry to the T+7 close price. Shows the full move independent of target/stop — useful for seeing how far the stock actually ran.
Stats bar

The stats bar at the top of the Simulation tab gives you a running scorecard across all closed trades:

  • 📊Win rate — percentage of closed trades that hit target or ended above entry at T+7. The backtested strategy achieves ~40.9% — anything above 40% over 20+ trades is on track.
  • 💰Avg P&L % — average T+7 percentage return across all closed trades. Because the reward (+2%) is twice the risk (−1%), a 40% win rate is still profitable in expectation.
  • 🏆Best / Worst — your highest and lowest T+7 P&L across all closed trades. Good for spotting outliers.
Why use the simulation before trading real money?
Running 20–30 paper trades first lets you see how the strategy actually performs on live forward data — not just backtest data. It also builds confidence and helps you understand how often trades timeout vs hit target vs stop. The backtested 40.9% win rate is a long-run average; short runs of 10–15 trades will vary widely.

Chart Controls
  • 📅 Range buttons (1D / 3D / 7D / 1M / 3M / 6M / 1Y) — snap the view to the last N trading days. The price change at the top updates to match the selected range.
  • 🔍 Scroll to zoom — mouse wheel zooms in/out centred on your cursor position. Zoom in to see individual ticks, zoom out for the big picture.
  • 📏 Drag to measure — click and drag on the chart to measure any segment. A floating box shows the +change / −change, percentage, and the exact date range. Click anywhere to clear it.
  • 🕯️ Candles — switches from a line chart to candlestick view. Use the candle size selector (Auto / 5m / 15m / 1H / 4H / 1D) to control the timeframe of each candle.
  • 📊 Volume — toggles the volume bar chart below the price chart. The y-axis uses the 95th percentile as its max so closing-auction spikes don't crush the scale.
  • 🔄 Reset Zoom — returns to the full data range in one click.
〰️
Moving Averages (MA20 / MA50 / MA200)

Toggle any combination of moving average overlays directly on the price or candlestick chart.

  • ━ MA20 20-day — short-term trend. Useful for spotting momentum shifts over 3–4 weeks.
  • ━ MA50 50-day — medium-term trend. The key reference for most swing strategies. Price above MA50 = bullish bias.
  • ━ MA200 200-day — long-term trend. The "golden cross" (MA50 crossing above MA200) is a classic bull signal.
MAs are calculated as sliding windows across all loaded data, not just the visible range — so they stay accurate even when zoomed in.
📉
RSI Indicator

Toggle the RSI (Relative Strength Index, 14-period) panel below the volume chart using the RSI button in the controls row.

  • 🔴RSI > 70 — overbought zone. The stock may be due for a pullback. Highlighted in red.
  • 🟢RSI < 30 — oversold zone. Potential reversal opportunity. Highlighted in green.
  • ⚪RSI 50 line — acts as the bull/bear dividing line. Sustained RSI above 50 = bullish momentum.
RSI is calculated using Wilder's smoothing (the original method), which produces smoother results than a simple average and is the industry standard.
🕯️
Pattern Engine

Click Patterns in the controls row to activate the candlestick pattern detection engine. It detects 60+ patterns (single, double, triple, and multi-candle formations) and projects forward probabilities.

Detection TF
Auto picks the best candle size for your current zoom. Override with 5m, 15m, 1H, 4H, or 1D to lock the timeframe.
Forecast horizon
Select 1D / 3D / 7D / 14D / 30D. Probability decays over time — the edge halves every 5 trading days, so shorter horizons are always more reliable.
Chart markers
▲ Green triangles = bullish patterns · ▼ Red triangles = bearish · ◆ Grey diamond = neutral.
Base win rate
Each pattern has a historical base probability (50–72%). The projected % for each horizon is shown next to the pattern name.
Context Filters

Activate one or more context filters to adjust pattern probabilities based on the current market regime. Filters are independent — use any combination.

  • 〰️ MA Trend — adjusts probability based on whether price is above/below MA50 and whether MA50 is sloping up or down. Bullish pattern above a rising MA50: ×1.12. Bullish pattern below a falling MA50: ×0.88.
  • 📉 RSI — bullish patterns with RSI in the 30–55 "sweet spot" get a boost (×1.10). Overbought RSI >70 reduces bullish probability (×0.85).
  • ✖️ MA Cross — bull market regime (MA50 above MA200): bullish patterns boosted ×1.08. Bear regime (MA50 below MA200): bullish patterns reduced ×0.92.
When any filter is active, an amber ⚡ Context filters active notice appears above the results listing which filters are on and that probabilities have been adjusted. All multipliers are clamped to [50%, 95%] so no pattern appears certain.

Alerts

Set a browser notification for a stock. Click Set Alert on any stock page. Alerts fire once and are checked on each auto-refresh cycle.

  • ⬆️Price above X — fires when the latest price crosses above a fixed level.
  • ⬇️Price below X — fires when the latest price drops below a fixed level.
  • 📈Intraday rise ≥ X% — compares the current price to today's opening price. Fires when the intraday gain reaches your threshold.
  • 📉Intraday drop ≥ X% — same but for intraday losses.
Browser notifications must be allowed for localhost:8765. If you don't see a permission prompt, check your browser's site settings.

Settings
  • 🔁 Stock page auto-refresh — how often the chart updates while you're viewing a stock. Set to Manual only to disable.
  • 🗓️ Auto-update schedule — the server refreshes all stocks automatically: every 15 min during market hours (9–17), every 60 min off-hours, every 4 hours on weekends.
  • 🔇 Disable auto-updates locally — uncheck "Enable automatic background updates" to stop the scheduler and startup refresh from running. Useful when developing locally so the server doesn't hammer the API. The server's own setting is independent.
  • 🎯 Update All scope — the nav bar "Update All" button only refreshes instrument types and currencies that are checked in Settings. "Update Everything" always refreshes all stocks regardless.
  • 🔢 Trades list limit — how many recent trades to show in the trades table on each stock page (10–50).

Quick Tips
  • 💡Use the Heatmap view on the dashboard for a fast overview of which sectors are moving — size = % magnitude.
  • 💡Zoom into a 1-day range and enable Candles + MA20 to see intraday momentum relative to the short-term average.
  • 💡Enable RSI + MA Trend context filter on the pattern engine for higher-quality signals — sweet-spot RSI (30–55) with price above MA50 is the strongest bullish setup.
  • 💡The measure tool (drag on chart) is great for quickly checking how much a stock moved between two specific dates or price levels.
  • 💡Star your top watchlist stocks then use the Favorites tab as your daily starting point instead of the full OMXS30.
  • 💡The Strategy ★ tab is most useful 30–60 min after market open when the DoD move is confirmed. Early morning picks can still reverse.
  • 💡Use the Simulation tab to validate picks from the Strategy tab before committing real capital — run it for a few weeks to see how the strategy performs live.
  • 💡Full History + MA200 is the fastest way to see if a stock is in a long-term bull or bear trend.
